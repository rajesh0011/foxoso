Why Choose Foxoso Hotels For Your Corporate Events

Diverse Accommodation Options

With 83 luxurious rooms, villas, and cottages in Goa and 34 deluxe rooms, suites, and retreats in Bhowali, the resorts can comfortably host large corporate groups and residential conferences.
Exceptional Event Support

Dedicated event management teams ensure seamless execution with state-of-the-art facilities like backup power, DJ arrangements, and curated team-building activities.
Idyllic Locations

The resorts' stunning beach and mountain locations in Goa and Bhowali offer a refreshing escape from the city, fostering creativity and team bonding in tranquil natural surroundings.

Exclusive Beachfront Venue

Offers stunning beachfront event space. A 40,000 sq ft lawn with direct beach access is perfect for corporate gatherings for up to 1,500 guests.

Expansive Banquet Facilities

Our versatile event venues and state-of-the-art conference facilities, can accommodate gatherings of all sizes.

Memorable Experiences

From team-building exercises to gala dinners, our dedicated event team will ensure your corporate event is a resounding success.
